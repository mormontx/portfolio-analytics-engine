Methodology: Projections use a closed-form Geometric Brownian Motion (GBM) model: S(t) = S₀ · exp[(μ − ½σ²)t + σ√t · Φ⁻¹(p)], where μ is the momentum-adjusted daily drift (50% long-term / 50% 20-day recent), σ is historical daily volatility, and Φ⁻¹(p) is the inverse normal CDF at the specified percentile. Bull = 90th, Base = 50th (median), Bear = 10th percentile. These are statistical projections, not price targets.
